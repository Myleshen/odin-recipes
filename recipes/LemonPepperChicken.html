Lemon Pepper Chicken II

Description

Easy, absolutely delicious lemon pepper chicken for the grill or oven.

Ingredients

  • â…” cup dry lemonade mix
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. To Marinate: In a nonporous glass dish or bowl combine the lemonade mix, pepper and salad dressing.
    Mix well. Add chicken and toss to coat; cover and refrigerate for 8 to 12 hours to marinate.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Remove chicken from marinade, discarding any remaining marinade, and place in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 35 to 40 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and juices run clear.
